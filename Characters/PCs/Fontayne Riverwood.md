# Background
## Father
His father [Philipo Riverwood, 58] is a rich and charismatic trader who always put work first. He has a large trading company in the city of [[Sintara]] and trades wares in almost anything. From the outside an ambitious man, but actually quite close connections with city/government officials and also ties to the corrupt underworld. Made a fortune many years ago by trading weapons etc to both sides of [xxx conflict]. 
Not a family man although he says he is. Has another younger child (m, 13, Hannes) and married to another (human) woman (36, f, Marrion).
Although Fontayne was for a large part raised by her, she never cared to much for him and put all he attention to his half-brother. She does pottery for a hobby because doesn't have to work and is mostly a socialite at her husbands parties, dinners and events. 
Fontayne has always bonded more with the housekeeping in their mansion (a halfling and an orc lady for housekeeping, and an dwarven chef).
Philipo has always wanted Fontayne to take over/support his business but also feels that this will not happen due to lack of ambition and eagerness. His father thinks its also best for business is Fontayne doesnt take-over and is somewhat annoyed because of that. Still supports him financially if needed, but has no further interest to bond.

# Mother
His mother (Fayvre Firwish) is wood elf from Grugach, Greyhawk. She left the city only 1.5y after Fontayne was born, back to Grugach far away. Fontayne met her two times when she visited the city when he was younger. Feels like they could bond more, but his mother is somewhat mysterious for him. Doesn't know what she does for living, or romantically, but he feels she is off well. 
Father said that she left because she couldn't keep up with his business. He feels like she would not be able to raise Fontayne with enough resources and safety due to her somewhat chaotic nature. Fontayne feels this is not true but cannot determine that for sure.

Fontayne has always felt more attracted to explore the more magical nature of his mother's background. He feels he has deep down some of that natural magic inside him, but has never been able to intuitively channel that, and has always been slightly jealous of others who could.. 
When Fontayne was younger he met a wizard who accompanied a famous politician at his father's house. He was intrigued by that aura, charisma and mystery of the wizard, and that has never let him go. He doesn't know who that wizard is and where/what he does now.

## Inspiration for Magic
Fontayne has always been a good student and thus feels wizardry is something he would be able to master (ambition from his father's side). 
He is mostly driven by mastering magic from an educational (almost scientific) pov, but also because he has slight jealousy and respect for those who have natural magic. He doesn't have a greater goal (yet), although perhaps he wants to oppose his father's business practices because he feels the business is slightly wrong and corrupt with no greater morale, except for gaining power and making money. 
He is somewhat conflicted by that because he is proud of his father's success but not by the means (Fontayne knows to little details about his dad's business but he knows it's not all legal). 

He wants to avoid doing damage or hurt with his magic, but he know very well that the world is not safe and filled with evil that does not care for your wellbeing.

## Study Background
After graduating high/middle school Fontayne got the opportunity to join (as an intern / apprentice found through his father's connection) at the city's monastery to explore the divine (XXX religion?). Mostly worked there for fairly basics tasks and grew more averse of the hierarchy of the monastery (they focused a lot on gaining wealth from visitors and from unfair taxes instead of being humble and devote.) Fontayne learned a lot about organizations, history and divine/religious entities from the library. 

After a year at the monastery, went to [xxx] wizardry school (at age ~21?) and has felt very much at home there. A much more diverse range of characters around him. It has made him feel more estranged from his father's company and business. He struggles with that feeling because he is still thankful for his upbringing.

He is still a student of the school now, going towards to last phase of study. He is struggling with some subjects and topics and this frustrates him, as he never had any troubles with school work or studies before. Sucks at chemistry or something..

He can do some magic and spells but does not feel proficient at all, compared to how some of his peers are doing. This makes him somewhat insecure about his wizard and magic skills. He thinks it doesn't look cool when he casts magic because he's a bit awkward with the gesturing. 

He is now considering to quit the wizarding school because he has trouble in finding a purpose, for after his graduation. --> Open for a adventure to trail his magic in the real world.

Calm, considerate, character; thinks a lot before he speaks.

Interested in transmutation, illusion, conjuring and divination

## Motivations
Fontanyne wants to rebel his father's company (for morale reasons) but also wants his dad to be proud of him.

He wants to master wizardry because it feels it could connect him to his elven-side; and also because he feels it could make him be respected; as he looks up to natural magical beings (jealousy) and powerful wizards.

Fontayne is thinking of quiting the last part of his wizard school because he feels he is missing some real-life experience and has learned mostly by studying theory. He has no idea what to do after he would finish studying. 

He is slightly afraid of certain types of magic but doesnt like to say this/admit this. 
He is a bit racist towards Dragonborn. Not clear why (probably deeprooted elven racsism or something).
