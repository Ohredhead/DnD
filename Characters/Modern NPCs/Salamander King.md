Leader of the [[Silman]] [[Lizardfolk]] tribe living in the [[Hool Marshes]] near to [[Saltmarsh]].

[[Salamander King|Alslong]] is worldly for a lizardfolk. In his youth, he spent several years exploring the nearby marshes. He knows about the outside world but feigns ignorance in an attempt to catch the characters in a lie. He respects those who show him respect and treat his people as their equals, rather than as primitive fools to be mocked or patronized. His experience with humans tells him that they can be useful allies, but he also knows that they can be duplicitous. ^089c8a
