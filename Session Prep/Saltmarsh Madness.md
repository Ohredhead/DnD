# Description
The [[Silman]] [[Lizardfolk]] tribe are arming up as described in [[Danger at Dunwater]]. They have gotten help from the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in [[Sintara]], and a blundering Fontayne senior.

The [[Lizardfolk]] have reportedly raided smaller and more vulnerable merchants floating down the river. This has been tolerated by the [[The Denarii|Order of Silver]] and richer merchants of [[Sintara]] since their business is not impacted.
