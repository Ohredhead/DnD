# Description
The [[Silman]] [[Lizardfolk]] tribe are planning a definitive raid on [[Saltmarsh]]. They have gotten help from the [[Cult of the Dragon]] in [[Sintara]], and an blundering Fontayne senior.

The [[Lizardfolk]] have long raided smaller and more vulnerable merchants floating down the river [[]]. This has long been tolerated by the [[The Denarii|Order of Silver]] and richer merchants of [[Sintara]] since their business is not impacted. Smaller merchants have built up resentment towards the elite as. a result but are not organized to act on the unrest any time soon.

Recently, the [[Lizardfolk]] have started targeting larger merchant companies, emboldened by new [[Lizard Bezerker]]s in their midst and additional [[Lizardfolk Shaman]] that have not been seen before in the area. The players can learn of this information by asking around in [[Saltmarsh]] or specifically inquiring about the [[Lizardfolk]] ambushes in [[Sintara]].
