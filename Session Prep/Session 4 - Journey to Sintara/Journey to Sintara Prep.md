# Background
In [[Session 2]] the squad cleared [[DnD/Locations/Gildani/Gildani Mine|Gildani Mine]] of the fungus. They found an entrance to a cave behind the mine. In [[Session 3]] they explored the cave and found a circle with intense evil magic that could be seen with detect magic. Also, a dias was on a raised part of the cave that had lingering magical traces on it, but was itself not magical. The group ended the session at the hidden entrance to the cave behind the waterfall.

## Hidden Info
The group does not know that the cave was being used by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] to turn [[Dragon Eggs]] into evil dragons. The rituals used the energy in the [[Armonite Crystals]] to perform their [[Nullification Ritual]]
