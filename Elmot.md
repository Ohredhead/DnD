Once a thriving metropolis it is now a warren of ruins favored by adventurers, thieves, and outcasts alike. Old customs and new blend as the ruins still thrum with life at the intersection of the current [[Selnor]], [[Ptilmen]], and [[Lortan]] kingdoms.
