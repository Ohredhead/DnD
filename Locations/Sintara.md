# Description
[[The Golden Capital]], [[Sintara]], is guarded to the North by the [[Maureen Mountains]], to the South and West the [[Cardile Ocean]], while the [[Misty Forest]] surrounds it on all sides but the east. The city is the capital of [[Selnor]] and is famed as a merchants paradise; being the main point of trade to the [[Southern Continent]], [[Aldurin]]. Here the [[Coinmaiden]], [[Our Lady of Gold]], [[Waukeen]] is revered above all. Her golden lions guard the [[Heraldic Gates]] and all who enter must pay the tithe of 1 gold coin.

Notably, the city is always bathed in the light of the [[Eternal Sun]].

## Organizations
- [[The Denarii]], or [[Order of Silver]], are the political and religious leaders of [[Sintara]] and the city. They are organized as a Merchant Republic, where the Merchant Houses vie for authority and wealth; one begetting the other.
