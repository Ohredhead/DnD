# Description
This rocky outcropping sits atop a submerged fortress occupied by the fearsome [[sahuagin]]. This place is fully detailed in [[The Final Enemy]]

This lair is an underwater stronghold maintained by the [[sahuagin]]. From here, they prey on passing ships and plot to grow in strength to the point that they can lay waste to [[Saltmarsh]]. A total of thirty [[sahuagin]], a [[Sahuagin High Priestess|sahuagin priestess]], and a [[sahuagin baron]] lair here.
