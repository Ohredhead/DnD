# Description
>A single cot rests by the south wall, an iron chest set at its foot. A large wooden carving of a lizardfolk brandishing a club hangs on the east wall.

The key to the chest in this room is hidden in [[LfL 18) Senior Shaman’s Living Quarters|area 18]]. The wooden carving on the wall is of no value.
# Chest
The chest is locked and can be opened by the key found in [[LfL 18) Senior Shaman’s Living Quarters|area 18]] or by a character who succeeds on a DC 12 Dexterity check using thieves' tools. The chest is trapped with a poison needle. Noticing the trap requires a successful DC 12 Intelligence (Investigation) check. A successful DC 12 Dexterity check is required to disarm the trap. Opening the chest without disarming the trap, or failing to disarm it, triggers the trap.

When triggered, the trap fires a small poisoned dart from the lock. Any creature standing in front of the lock is automatically struck by the needle, taking 1 piercing damage and 4 (1d4 + 2) poison damage.

# Treasure
The chest contains the personal wealth of the senior shaman: a silver figure of [[Semuanya]] (25 gp), a leather bag containing 50 ep, and a [pearl of power](https://5e.tools/items.html#pearl%20of%20power_dmg).
