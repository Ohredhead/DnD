# Description
>Thirty feet beyond the gate is a bare room with a wooden bench set against the east wall.

A [[Lizardfolk Render]] and a [[Lizardfolk]] occupy this area, ordered to stand guard over the lair's treasure ([[LfL 26) Gate and Treasure Room|area 26]]). They do not open the gate unless commanded to do so by the [[Salamander King|King]] or the [[Irhtos|subchief]].

The guards in this area keep a watch on the gate and the secret door that leads to [[LfL 26) Gate and Treasure Room|area 26]]. They defend the gate if they hear anyone trying to attack it, but a successful attempt at unlocking it does not draw their attention.
