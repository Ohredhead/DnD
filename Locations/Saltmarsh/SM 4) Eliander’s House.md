# Description
Tucked at the edge of town and overlooking the sea, [[Eliander Fireborn|Eliander]]'s home provides him with a relaxing sanctuary away from the bustle of [[Saltmarsh]]. [[Eliander Fireborn|Eliander]] maintains the largest library in town; during his days of military service, he made a hobby of collecting rare books. If the characters need information on the history of [[Saltmarsh]], they might find it in [[Eliander Fireborn|Eliander]]'s archives.
