# Background
The [[Cult of the Dragon]] has been active in [[Tarcia]] for centuries. It has focused on opening portals to [[Tiamat]] in order channel power from [[Tiamat|Her]] realm.

## Leader
The new leader of the cult is the [[Calishite]] named [[Severin Silrajin]] who believes that real draconian knowledge and power belongs to living dragons.
