A ritual performed by the [[Cult of the Dragon]] to corrupt metallic [[Dragon Eggs]].

They use a locally available energy source to rip open a rift to the plane of [[Carceri]]. Once complete [[Null]] has set up a channel of energy from [[Tiamat]] in [[Avernus]]. This slowly corrupts the eggs but often allows other creatures and entities to come through the rift.

### What can come through the rifts?
- [[Spore Core]]s, [[Demodand]]s, [[Shator]]s, [[Nightmare]]s, [[Achaierai]], [[Shadow Demon]]s, [[Mephit]]s, [[Hordling]]s, [[Vargouille]]
